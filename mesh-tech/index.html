ABOUT US

Our History

MeshTech Resources was founded on the 18 December 2015 in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria. Since then, the company has grown both in size and reputation. The company executes projects in Port Harcourt, Aba, Uyo, Auchi, Calabar, Enugu, Owerri and Umuahia Respectively. MeshTech Resources was officially Incorporated in 2020, As a corporate entity, we have a broad portfolio of successfully completed projects across a range of sectors including Residential, Worship, Commercial and Educational. Projects such as Residential Building developments, Church, Rent Apartments Class Room Buildings and Shopping Plazas are areas we have excelled over the years. As building contractors and developers, MeshTech Resources is well equipped to undertake complex technical projects and to manage their works from design to completion. MeshTech Resources, we believe that the success of any project commences with understanding individual strength and the formation of a compatible committed and well-resourced construction team that understands the requirements and anticipated outcome.

Vision

Our vision is to be the number one indigenous construction company, creating value and incomparable construction techniques through acceptable standards, hence providing excellent project delivery within timely period.

Mission

MeshTech Resources is committed to building long-term relationships based on Integrity, Performance, Value Creation and Client Satisfaction by maintaining the highest level of professionalism, fairness in relationship with our Suppliers, Subcontractors, Employees, Professional Associates and Clients.

Quality Assurance

MeshTech Resources is proud of the quality of work it delivers to its clients; the company ensures the use of quality materials throughout its projects regardless of size, we believe in doing that we would set and maintain standards hence, keep our clients and also attract potential clients. It is the company’s motto: "Build Right” this is integral in every aspect of our process. We are determined to continue our incredible path to growth through delivering quality projects no matter the circumstance, because we believe that in order to attain excellence that is the only way forward.

Health and Safety.

MeshTech Resources takes the health and safety very paramount, the company is committed and responsible for the health and safety of all members of staff and general public within our office and sites. Health and safety is installed subconsciously, and also a constant reminder by health and safety professionals of its significance to our workers both in the office and onsite. Therefore it is part of the company’s policy to provide a healthy and safe working environment with adequate provision for safety wears to avoid injuries, occupational hazards and first aid box in case of any emergency. Furthermore, the company ensures strict adherence to its health and safety policy. However, the company expects responsible behaviour from all workers through visible commitment and active engagement. Achieving this will ensure and underline success ultimately.

Design

MeshTech Resources understands the importance of your space so it's dedicated in-house resources to manage the design process on the delivery of our projects. We work closely with our preferred external design consultants or Clients to develop, coordinate and manage innovative and cost effective solutions for our clients whilst ensuring the design brief, specifications and public procurement requirements are met from master-plan or concept design stage right through to construction documentation. Our design teams are adept at managing the process MeshTech Resources believes outstanding projects are conceived via a collaborative, creative and disciplined design management process. This process establishes a culture of innovation, listening and team work. 90% of our projects involve managing part or all of the design, depending on the procurement method and requirements of our clients.

Construction

MeshTech Resources has successfully delivered a diverse range of construction projects for both private and public sectors. We have developed loyal business relationships through servicing all our client projects whether large or small. MeshTech Resources has constructed projects over the years and covered various industry sectors of residential, commercial, worship, education. Our construction efforts ensure that our projects meet industry best practice standards in terms of quality, safety, and environmental management. Our facilities and equipment procurement are state of the art of the highest standard to deliver the project. We take into account the safety standards of every equipment to protect our employees and clients from threatening hazards. Our construction teams are supported by an extensive network of subcontract and supplier organizations ensuring we can introduce the latest construction techniques and materials to our projects. Our subcontractor and supplier base also ensures that we can source the best trade and material prices available in the market. This cost efficiency is passed on to MeshTech Resources clients in the form of competitive tender prices.
